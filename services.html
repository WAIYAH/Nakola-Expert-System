FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How much does a typical project cost?

Costs vary depending on complexity, timeline, and scope. MVPs typically range from $10K–$30K, full web applications from $25K–$80K, and enterprise platforms from $80K–$500K+. We always provide a detailed proposal with fixed pricing before work begins — no surprise bills.

How long does a typical project take?

MVPs can be delivered in 4–8 weeks. Most full-featured applications take 3–6 months. Enterprise projects with complex integrations may take 6–12 months. We provide clear timelines and milestones during the Discovery phase.

Do you work with clients outside Africa?

Absolutely. While we're headquartered in Nairobi, we serve clients globally. Our distributed team works across time zones, and we use tools like Slack, Notion, and Loom to maintain seamless async communication. We've delivered projects for clients in the US, UK, UAE, and across Europe.

What makes you different from other dev agencies?

Founder-led delivery — every project has direct senior oversight. We combine African market understanding with global engineering standards. You won't be handed off to juniors or lost in a queue. Plus, we measure success by your business outcomes, not just code shipped.

What happens after the project launches?

Every project includes a 30-day warranty period for bug fixes. After that, we offer flexible Managed Support plans starting at $500/month. We also provide complete documentation and knowledge transfer so you're never locked in.
