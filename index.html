About Us

Leading Innovator in African Tech

Founded in 2024 by Lucky Loonkishu Nakola in Nairobi, Kenya, Nakola Expert Systems has rapidly grown into a powerhouse in the African tech landscape. We specialize in delivering cutting-edge IT and software solutions tailored to the unique challenges and opportunities in Kenya and across Africa. Our mission is to provide practical, intelligent, and scalable tools that drive growth, efficiency, and progress for businesses, communities, and institutions. With a focus on AI, software development, and digital transformation, we are committed to empowering Africa through technology.
