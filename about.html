About Us

Built in Africa. Delivering Globally.

Nakola Expert Systems was founded on a simple conviction: African engineering talent, when backed by the right leadership and processes, can compete with anyone in the world.

Our Story

From a Bold Idea to a Trusted Partner

Nakola Expert Systems was born from a frustration: too many businesses were forced to choose between affordable but unreliable local developers and expensive but distant international agencies.

We set out to bridge that gap — combining deep understanding of African markets with engineering practices honed at global standards. Every project gets founder-level attention, not a junior fresh out of a bootcamp.

Today, we serve clients across 8+ countries, from Nairobi-based startups to pan-African enterprises and international organizations. Our mission hasn't changed: deliver technology that measurably moves the needle for our clients' businesses.

Mission & Vision

Our Vision

Our Values

Principles That Guide Every Decision

These aren't wall posters. They're the actual standards we hold ourselves to on every project, every sprint, every conversation.

Outcomes Over Output

We measure success by your business results — revenue growth, efficiency gains, user adoption — not by lines of code shipped or hours billed.

Radical Transparency

No hidden costs, no surprise timelines, no black-box processes. You see everything — code, progress, challenges — in real time.

Senior-Led, Always

Every project has experienced leadership from kickoff to handover. You will never be "handed off" to juniors mid-project.

African-Global Mindset

We build for African realities — low bandwidth, mobile-first, local payments — while maintaining standards that work in London, Lagos, and Nairobi.

Continuous Learning

Technology evolves fast. We invest in our team's growth weekly — new frameworks, architectural patterns, and industry best practices.

Long-Term Partnership

We're not a vendor that disappears after launch. Our best relationships are multi-year partnerships where we grow alongside our clients.
