About Nakola Expert Systems

Who We Are

Nakola Expert Systems is a Nairobi-based technology leader, founded in 2024, dedicated to transforming the African digital landscape. We specialize in delivering innovative, scalable, and culturally relevant IT and AI solutions that empower businesses, communities, and institutions across Kenya and beyond.

Our Story

Established in 2024 by visionary entrepreneur Lucky Loonkishu Nakola, Nakola Expert Systems emerged from a passion for harnessing technology to address Africa’s unique challenges. From a dynamic startup, we’ve grown into a recognized powerhouse, delivering over a dozen high-impact projects in sectors like fintech, education, and healthcare. Our journey is fueled by a commitment to innovation, client-centric solutions, and empowering African communities through technology.

Vision & Mission

Vision

To become the leading tech solutions provider in Africa, delivering scalable, innovative, and impactful systems tailored to local contexts and global standards.

Mission

To deliver practical, intelligent software and AI-powered tools that solve real-world problems for businesses, communities, and institutions in Kenya and across Africa, fostering growth, efficiency, and progress.
