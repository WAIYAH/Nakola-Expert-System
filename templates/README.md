# Templates System README

## Overview
This `/templates` directory converts the Nakola Expert Systems website content into reusable business documentation for sales, operations, branding, legal preparedness, and client delivery workflows.

## Folder Structure
- `business-documents/`
  - Core commercial and operational templates: company profile, letterhead, quotations, invoices, receipts, and proposals.
- `client-documents/`
  - Client delivery templates: onboarding, project brief, requirements collection, handover, and maintenance agreement.
- `branding/`
  - Brand communication templates: brand guidelines, email signature, business card content, and social media bios.
- `legal/`
  - Legal/trust templates: service agreement, privacy policy, terms and conditions, and NDA.
- `marketing/`
  - Growth templates: service packages, outreach email, WhatsApp message flows, case study format, and capability statement.

## How To Use
1. Start with `CONTENT_AUDIT.md` to confirm official business data and messaging.
2. Duplicate the template you need and rename it for the specific client or transaction.
3. Replace placeholders such as `[Client Name]`, `[Project Name]`, `[Company Registration Number]`, and `[KRA PIN]`.
4. Keep pricing, legal terms, and compliance clauses aligned to signed agreements.

## Placeholder Rules
- Do not invent official records that are not yet published.
- Use placeholders until verified by leadership or finance.
- Common placeholders include:
  - `[Company Registration Number]`
  - `[KRA PIN]`
  - `[Registered Address]`
  - `[Authorized Signatory Name]`
  - `[Project Name]`
  - `[Client Name]`

## Legal Review Requirement
All files in `legal/` are templates only. They must be reviewed and approved by a qualified legal professional before official use.

## Recommended Internal Workflow
1. Sales prepares quotation/proposal templates.
2. Delivery team completes client brief and requirements templates.
3. Operations/finance issue invoice and receipt templates.
4. Legal/compliance validates contractual documents.
5. Marketing team reuses capability and case study templates for growth.
