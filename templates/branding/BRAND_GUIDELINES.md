# Brand Guidelines Template

## 1. Brand Identity
- Brand Name: **Nakola Expert Systems**
- Primary Tagline: **Build Smarter. Scale Faster. Innovate Globally.**
- Alternate Tagline: **Built in Africa. Delivering Globally.**
- Core Positioning: African-grounded technical expertise with global-standard delivery.

## 2. Brand Promise
We help ambitious organizations transform ideas into reliable digital products with measurable business outcomes.

## 3. Brand Voice
- Confident and professional
- Strategic and business-outcome focused
- Transparent and collaborative
- Technically credible without unnecessary jargon

## 4. Messaging Pillars
1. Measurable outcomes over vanity output
2. Senior-led delivery and accountability
3. African market understanding with global execution standards
4. Long-term partnership mindset

## 5. Color Palette
Primary brand colors (from current frontend system):
- Primary Blue: `#2563eb`
- Accent Purple: `#7c3aed`

Supporting colors:
- Success Green: `#10b981`
- Premium Gold: `#f59e0b`
- Teal Accent: `#14b8a6`
- Neutral Scale: `#f9fafb` to `#030712`

Usage guidance:
- Use blue as primary action color
- Use blue-purple gradients for hero and premium highlights
- Use green for success states and positive metrics
- Keep dark neutrals for contrast and enterprise tone

## 6. Typography
- Heading Font: `Inter`
- Body Font: `Inter`
- Monospace Accent: `JetBrains Mono`

Usage guidance:
- Headings: bold and concise
- Body: clear and readable, business-friendly
- Monospace: technical snippets or data callouts
