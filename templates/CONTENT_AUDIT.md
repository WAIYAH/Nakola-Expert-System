## Missing Information (Use Placeholders in Business Docs)
The website does not publish the following official business records, so templates should keep placeholders:
- Company registration number
- Tax/KRA PIN
- Legal signatory details
- Banking/payment account details
- Physical street address (beyond city/country)
- Jurisdiction clause details for contracts

Recommended placeholders:
- `[Company Registration Number]`
- `[KRA PIN]`
- `[Registered Address]`
- `[Authorized Signatory Name]`
- `[Authorized Signatory Title]`
- `[Bank Name]`, `[Bank Account Number]`, `[SWIFT Code]`
- `[Governing Law/Jurisdiction]`
